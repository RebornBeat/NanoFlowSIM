### NFSIM-ENV-02 — Water Pathogen Station

**README.** Automated water sample → LAMP detection station for E. coli, Salmonella, Cryptosporidium, etc. Continuous water monitoring for wells, schools, food processing.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** Automated sample injection + filter concentration + LAMP detection chamber. 90 min sample-to-result. ~$145.

**THEORY.** Concentrates pathogens from large water volume (10 L) onto filter; LAMP reaction detects amplicon.

**ASSEMBLY.** Pump + filter housing + LAMP chamber + heater + optics.
