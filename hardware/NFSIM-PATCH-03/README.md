### NFSIM-PATCH-03 — Iontophoresis Drug Delivery Patch

**README.** Active drug delivery patch using iontophoresis (small DC current drives charged drugs across skin). Pilocarpine sweat induction, lidocaine pre-procedure anesthesia, glucocorticoid local delivery.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** Programmable current 0.1–4 mA × 1–60 min; safety interlock (skin impedance + current monitoring); BLE control. ~$110.

**THEORY.** Iontophoresis: applied DC current drives ionizable drug molecules through skin into local tissue. Faster than passive transdermal; controllable dose.

**ASSEMBLY.** Two electrodes (anode + cathode); drug-containing hydrogel pad; current source with safety limits.
