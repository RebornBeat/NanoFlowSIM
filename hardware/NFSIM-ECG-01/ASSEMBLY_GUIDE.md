#### ASSEMBLY_GUIDE.md

**Time:** ~3 hours per first patch. **Skills:** SMD, biocompatible packaging.

**Step 1 — PCB.** Hybrid flex-rigid 2-layer flex + 4-layer rigid. JLCPCB PCBA.

**Step 2 — Assemble.** Pick-and-place via JLCPCB. Hand-solder battery wires.

**Step 3 — Flash firmware.** Nordic SWD via tag-connect cable.

**Step 4 — Calibrate ADC.** Apply known 1 mV / 10 Hz test signal; verify ADC counts match expected.

**Step 5 — Encapsulate.** TPE overmold via casting (silicone mold). Cure 24h.

**Step 6 — Attach adhesive + electrodes.** 3M 1585N adhesive base on patch back; hydrogel electrodes attached separately.

**Step 7 — Validate.** Wear test on volunteer; record 24h of ECG; verify event detection on intentional irregular rhythms (mild caffeine + sleep deprivation for some PVCs in volunteers willing to test).

**Step 8 — Long-wear test.** First batch: 30-day continuous wear on engineering team volunteers. Monitor battery, skin irritation, false-positive rate.
