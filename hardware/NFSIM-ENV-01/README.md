### NFSIM-ENV-01 — Bio-Aerosol Sampler

**README.** Cyclone-based air sampler + automated LAMP/PCR reaction chamber. Detects airborne pathogens (TB, SARS-CoV-2, influenza, fungal spores) for indoor air monitoring, hospital surveillance, biosecurity.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** Cyclone separator + microfluidic capture into 1 mL reaction chamber + automated LAMP + fluorescence detection. 60-90 min sample-to-result. ~$95.

**THEORY.** Cyclone concentrates airborne particles into liquid; LAMP amplification + fluorescence detection (same chemistry as PCR-03).

**BLOCK DIAGRAM.** Cyclone + brushless pump + microfluidic chamber + PCR-03-style fluorescence reader.

**ASSEMBLY.** CNC cyclone separator; PDMS microfluidic; integrate with PCR-03 chamber.
