### NFSIM-CAP-06 — pH / Pressure / Motility (SmartPill alternative)

**README.** Wireless GI motility capsule measuring pH, pressure, temperature. GI transit time, gastric emptying, bowel motility assessment.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** ISFET pH + BMP581 (pressure) + NTC + nRF52805 + Si4463. 80 mAh. 72h life. ~$185.

**THEORY.** Same principle as Medtronic SmartPill (now discontinued in some markets). Pressure detects peristaltic contractions; pH localizes the capsule to GI segment.

**ASSEMBLY.** Pressure port + pH sensor exposed through capsule wall with biocompatible coating.
