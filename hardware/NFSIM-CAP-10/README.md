### NFSIM-CAP-10 — GI Bleeding Detection (dual-wavelength Hb)

**README.** Hemoglobin detection capsule using dual-wavelength (660 + 940 nm) optical absorbance — same physics as pulse oximetry. Detects occult blood in GI tract for screening colorectal cancer.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** Dual LED + photodiode + custom optical cell + nRF52840. ~$120.

**THEORY.** Hb has different absorbances at 660 vs. 940 nm; ratio independent of optical path. Optical absorbance through luminal fluid identifies blood presence.

**ASSEMBLY.** Optical cell with controlled path length + dual LEDs.
