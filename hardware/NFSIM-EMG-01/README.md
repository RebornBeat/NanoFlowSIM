### NFSIM-EMG-01 — 8-Channel EMG Armband

**README.** Wearable armband with 8 EMG channels for gesture recognition + neuromuscular monitoring. Myoware-style + on-device gesture ML.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** ADS1298 8-channel @ 1 kHz; dry/textile electrodes; on-device gesture classifier (TFLite Micro). BLE 5.0. ~$115.

**THEORY.** Surface EMG captures motor unit activation patterns. ML classifies hand gestures, grip strength, fatigue.

**ASSEMBLY.** Stretchable armband with embedded electrode rings around forearm.
