### NFSIM-CON-01 — Sample Prep Disc

**README.** Centrifugal microfluidic disc for plasma/serum separation, cell isolation, nucleic acid extraction. Disposable single-use.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** Polycarbonate disc with embedded channels + mixing chambers + reaction wells; spun on a small benchtop motor; centrifugal force drives fluid through channels. ~$55 (disc + drive motor unit).

**THEORY.** Centrifugal microfluidics: chambers + valves activated by rotational speed; precise fluid handling without external pumps. Common pattern for sample prep automation.

**ASSEMBLY.** Injection-molded disc + small ~$5 spinner motor unit.
