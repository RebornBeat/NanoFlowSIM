### NFSIM-NEURAL-02 — Intracortical Headstage

**README.** Open Ephys-compatible intracortical recording headstage. For Utah array / silicon probe recording in research models.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** Intan RHD2132 (32ch); SPI to Open Ephys acquisition box; designed for Utah array Cereport connector or NeuroNexus pinout. ~$380.

**THEORY.** Single-unit and LFP recording from intracortical probes.

**ASSEMBLY.** Compact PCB attached to commercial intracortical array via Omnetics connector. Standard research equipment.
