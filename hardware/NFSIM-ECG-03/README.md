### NFSIM-ECG-03 — 3-Lead Holter Patch

**README.** Smaller 3-lead Holter alternative to 12-lead vest. 30-day or 7-day wear options.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** ADS1294 (4 channels) → 3 leads (LA-RA, LL-RA, LL-LA derived) + respiration impedance. nRF52840. 1000 mAh battery (30-day). ~$52.

**THEORY.** Three-lead bipolar derivation for higher diagnostic content than single-lead. Respiration impedance plethysmography from same electrodes.

**ASSEMBLY.** Similar to ECG-01 but with three electrode positions.
