### NFSIM-CON-03 — Transdermal Energy Mat

**README.** Through-tissue inductive power transmission mat (~1 MHz) for charging implantable devices without removal. Used with IMPLANT-01, IMPLANT-02.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** 1 MHz Tx coil + driver, ~5W deliverable through 2 cm tissue; user lies on or wears the mat for 10-30 min recharge sessions. ~$65.

**THEORY.** Inductive coupling at 1 MHz penetrates skin/subcutaneous tissue with reasonable efficiency.

**ASSEMBLY.** Flexible coil mat + driver electronics in compact housing.
