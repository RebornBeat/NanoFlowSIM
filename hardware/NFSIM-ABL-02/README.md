### NFSIM-ABL-02 — Portable Focused Ultrasound (HIFU)

**README.** Portable high-intensity focused ultrasound for thermal ablation (research/veterinary). HIFU + integrated thermal dose monitor (CEM43).

**SPECIFICATIONS.** 1.5 MHz focused transducer + 100W RF driver + thermistor-based dose monitor. Research-grade; NOT for clinical use without full regulatory approval. ~$340.

**THEORY.** Focused ultrasound delivers heat at depth (focal spot ~1mm³ at several cm depth) for non-invasive tissue ablation. Used for tumor ablation, palliative fibroid treatment, etc.

**SAFETY.** Mandatory dose monitoring (CEM43 = cumulative equivalent minutes at 43°C); auto-shutoff at safe limits.

**ASSEMBLY.** Custom HIFU transducer + RF amp + thermistor array. Research equipment.
