### NFSIM-DEL-02 — Drug Implant (Osmotic + NFC valve)

**README.** Subcutaneous osmotic drug implant with NFC-programmable valve. Slow-release pharmaceutical for hormone replacement, long-acting contraception, etc.

**SPECIFICATIONS.** Sealed implant (~3×30 mm) with osmotic chamber + drug chamber + NFC-controlled valve. ~$185.

**THEORY.** Water enters osmotic chamber, pushes drug out through cannula. Valve controls flow rate. NFC interrogation reads remaining drug + last dose timestamps.

**ASSEMBLY.** Custom precision-machined implantable form. USP Class VI. Sterile.
